Why this exists

I had a kid and basically forgot to drink water for 6 months. Lived on coffee. Felt terrible. Blamed the baby.

Turns out mild dehydration tanks your mood and energy. And that 4pm coffee was why I couldn't sleep when the baby finally did.

DadFuel doesn't do anything clever. It just shows you what you're actually consuming so you can make slightly better choices. That's enough.
